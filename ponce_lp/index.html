DC Metro Area's Premier Adult Baseball League

We have served men, ages 30+, for over 25 years.

Ponce de Leon Baseball League was founded in 1990 with the goal of providing men of all ages and skill levels the opportunity to play baseball. Ponce currently has 3 seasons (Spring, Summer, Fall) and offers a one-week Winter Camp (Fort Meyers, FL) for those interested in taking their game to the next level.

No matter your level of experience, you can rediscover your passion for baseball and play with Ponce De Leon!

Facing off against a veteran pitcher might seem imtimidating at first, but Ponce's unique set of rules allows all skill levels to contribute and have fun.

Nothing beats the feeling of stepping back onto the ballfield. Ponce teams play weekly on our area's beautiful park and high school fields. Ready to lace up your spikes yet?

Join other guys, just like you, who love the game of baseball. Ponce teams enjoy the camaraderie of their teammates - and the friendly competition of the opposing players.
